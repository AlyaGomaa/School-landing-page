In our school, We believe that rewarding students encourages and endorses school effort. That’s why we developed a house points tracking and reward system.

Welcome to Kipling School, As part of the non-profit Child and Society Development Institute and Smart Village Schools, Kipling offers an outstanding educational experience at an affordable cost. Students follow the English National Curriculum in conjunction with the Cambridge International Primary Curriculum leading to Cambridge Checkpoint, IGCSEs, AS and A levels. Graduating students can access both Egyptian and International universities and are fine examples of our vision to develop learners with an International perspective that respect Egyptian Heritage. Facilities are excellent and include state-of-the-art computer and science labs, well equipped music and art rooms as well as access to Smart Village’s sports club where students swim and enjoy physical exercise in a modern and safe environment.Most teachers hold UK post graduate certificates in education and all members of the teaching staff are committed to delivering high quality child centred teaching and learning. We firmly believe in the three Ts of teamwork, technology and thinking. Our students will be prepared for the challenges that a fast changing World will bring and become valued members of society.Through our House system (Air, Earth, Fire and Water) we motivate students to reach their full academic potential, encourage creativity, social responsibility and understanding of others.I urge you to visit us and see what makes Kipling a unique school.
